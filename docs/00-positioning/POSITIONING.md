# Product Design Positioning

## North Star

Cello is a product designer who bridges design and engineering through thoughtful prototyping, systems thinking, and clear product storytelling.

## Short Positioning Statement

Product Designer focused on design systems, technical prototyping, and design-dev collaboration.

## Longer Positioning Statement

I design product experiences and build prototypes that help designers, developers, and stakeholders make better product decisions faster.

My work combines UX/UI design, frontend development, design systems, CMS workflows, automation, and product storytelling.

## What Makes This Positioning Strong

This positioning connects several strengths:

- UX and UI design
- Communication and stakeholder collaboration
- Frontend development knowledge
- Full-stack awareness
- Design systems thinking
- Prototyping skill
- CMS and e-commerce experience
- Ability to translate between designers and developers

## What To Emphasize

Emphasize:

- Product design
- Systems thinking
- Prototyping
- Buildable design
- Technical product thinking
- Design-dev collaboration
- Cross-functional communication
- Design systems
- Product storytelling

## What To Reduce

Reduce:

- Generic "designer/developer" language
- Long tool lists without outcomes
- Overemphasis on WordPress as the identity
- Visual work without product framing
- Code projects without product context

## Suggested Headlines

Product Designer for systems, prototypes, and design-dev collaboration.

Product Designer focused on design systems, technical prototyping, and frontend-capable UX.

I design product experiences and prototypes that make ideas easier to test, explain, and build.

## Target Role Language

Use language that fits:

- Product Designer
- Technical Product Designer
- Design Systems Designer
- Product Designer, Developer Tools
- Product Designer, Design Tools
- Product Designer, CMS/Admin Tools
- UX/UI Product Designer
- Frontend-capable Product Designer
