# Labs And Experiments

## Goal

Use the Lab section for smaller prototypes, technical explorations, and design-dev experiments that do not yet need full case studies.

## Belongs In Lab

- Interaction prototypes
- Payload experiments
- Figma experiments
- React or Astro component explorations
- AI-assisted workflow tests
- CMS/admin workflow experiments
- Small product ideas

## Does Not Belong In Lab

- Finished flagship case studies
- Visual archive-only work
- Personal task tracking
- Raw code snippets without product context

## Entry Structure

Each lab entry should include:

- What I tested
- Why it mattered
- Tools used
- What worked
- What I learned
- What I would try next

## Relationship To Case Studies

Lab entries can become evidence for larger case studies. They can also remain small proof points that show curiosity, technical fluency, and product thinking.
